About Us Our Story

At our core, we are passionate about bringing you the best coffee experience. Located in the heart of Golden Valley, Minnesota, we have been serving our community with dedication and love for many years.

Our mission is simple: to create a space where coffee lovers can gather, enjoy a great cup of coffee, and feel at home. We carefully select our beans, craft each cup with precision, and ensure that every visit is a delightful experience.

From the moment you step into our café, you’ll be greeted with the aroma of freshly brewed coffee and the warmth of our friendly staff. Whether you're here for a quick coffee run or to relax with friends, we strive to make every visit special.

Strong Coffee, Strong Roots About Our Cafe

Founded in 1987 by the Hernandez brothers, our establishment has been serving up rich coffee sourced from artisan farmers in various regions of South and Central America. We are dedicated to travelling the world, finding the best coffee, and bringing back to you here in our cafe.

We guarantee that you will fall in lust with our decadent blends the moment you walk inside until you finish your last sip. Join us for your daily routine, an outing with friends, or simply just to enjoy some alone time.
